Benjamin Clawhauser

Clawhauser loves two things: pop star Gazelle and donuts. He greets everyone with a warm smile and a helpful paw covered in sprinkles.

  • Occupation: Police Officer
  • Species: Cheetah
  • Appearance: Black spots with yellow fur
  • Home: Zootopia
  • Quote: O...M...Goodness!

He is the opposite of what a traditional cheetah should look like, Clawhauser is very fat. He loves to stuff himself with donuts, Lucky Chomps cereal, acts of kindness, soda, eating junk food and enjoys pop star, Gazelle.
